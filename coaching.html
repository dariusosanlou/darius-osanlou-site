Background

Why I coach this way.

Before coaching, I worked in psychology, supporting people with anxiety and depression, and later as a medical writer in pharmaceuticals, interpreting clinical research for a living.

Throughout both, I kept noticing the same thing: people rarely failed because they didn't know enough. They failed because stress, habit, identity and circumstance kept pulling them back toward the person they were trying to change.

That's what Body Psychology is built around. Training and nutrition matter. But they work best when the person following them is changing too.

Many of my clients don't arrive with perfect health, perfect motivation or a clean slate. Some are managing chronic health conditions (type 1 diabetes, chronic fatigue, haemophilia, endometriosis), others are rebuilding after years of disordered eating or mental health difficulties. Coaching has to work in the real world, not just on paper.

Questions

Before you apply.

Is this just another diet plan?

No. Plans are the easy part. I coach the psychology, behaviour and physiology together, which is why the results last instead of unravelling in week seven.

I've failed before. Why would this be different?

Because we fix the reason you stopped, not just what you eat. Most programmes ignore the mental drivers entirely. That's the whole point of body psychology.

Do I need to already be training?

No. Some clients come to me already training consistently. Others haven't trained properly in years. The common factor isn't fitness level, it's a willingness to be honest about what's getting in the way.

Do you only work online?

Yes. 1:1, fully online, anywhere in the world. You get bespoke coaching and direct access to me without needing to be in the same city.

How do I get started?

The first step is a short application so I can understand where you're at and what you're trying to change. If it looks like a good fit, we'll have a conversation before anything else. Apply here.

How much time does it take each week?

Less than you think. The plan is built around your real schedule. The goal is something sustainable, not another thing you can't keep up.

Ready to stop starting over?

Coaching is application-only. If you're serious about doing this properly, start here.
