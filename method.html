The Method

Body psychology.

If information was enough, most people would already have the body they want.

The reason change is so difficult is that every new plan is being carried out by the same person, with the same habits, routines, beliefs, and patterns that created the problem in the first place.

That's why my coaching starts one level deeper.

Before we focus on changing your body, we focus on understanding the person responsible for the decisions that shape it.

Your habits.
Your mindset.
Your environment.
Your strengths.
Your blind spots.

Because lasting change doesn't come from finding the perfect plan.
It comes from becoming the person who can follow one.

Once that foundation is in place, training and nutrition become far more effective. Not because the plan is better, but because the person following it is.

That's what Body Psychology is.
